# My Journey to Becoming a Better Developer

Software engineering isn't just about writing code. It's about solving problems, learning from failure, and constantly pushing yourself to improve.

## Where It Started

I started coding at Everett Community College, grinding through data structures and algorithms. What kept me going wasn't talent - it was stubbornness.

## What I've Learned

1. **Build things that break.** You learn more from debugging than from tutorials.
2. **Read other people's code.** Open source taught me more than any class.
3. **Ship early.** A working prototype beats a perfect plan.

## Where I'm Going

I'm focused on distributed systems and AI infrastructure now. The problems are harder, the scale is bigger, and that's exactly what makes it exciting.
